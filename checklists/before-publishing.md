# Checklist: Before Publishing

- Confirm publication is approved.
- Confirm all examples are public or synthetic.
- Run private-data scan.
- Manually review every scan hit.
- Remove private project names unless explicitly approved.
- Include scope and limitations.
- Do not claim adoption or results that do not exist.
- Do not claim this replaces security, legal, compliance, or production review.
- Record what was published and where.
